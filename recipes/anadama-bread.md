# Anadama Bread
Yield: 4 loaves

## ingredients
* 2 Tbsp yeast
* 1 1/2 cups warm water
* 5 cups hot water
* 2 cups cornmeal
* 1 Tbsp salt
* 1 1/2 cups molasses
* 1/2 cup melted butter
* 4 cups whole wheat flour
* 10-12 cups bread flour (extra if needed)
* (1 egg for eggwash)

## steps  
1. Dissolve yeast in warm water.
2. In a separate bowl, combine hot water, cornmeal, and molasses. Let sit until cool enough to add yeast mixture.
3. Add salt, butter, all the whole wheat flour, and half of the bread flour.
4. Knead in mixer and add remaining bread flour until dough forms a smooth ball. Poke test.
5. Let rise until doubled in volume (1 or 2 hours).
6. Punch down and shape.
7. Brush with eggwash. Sprinkle cornmeal on top. Score with bread lame.
8. Allow to rise until poke test shows readiness.
9. Bake in 375°F oven until internal temp is 180°F or higher (approximately 30-40 minutes).

## notes  
* Oven temperature 375°F
